## What is Operations Research?
 
Operations research is a branch of mathematics that deals with the optimization of complex systems and processes. It aims to find the best possible solutions to problems that involve multiple objectives, constraints, variables, and uncertainties. Operations research can be applied to various fields and domains, such as engineering, management, economics, logistics, health care, military, and more.
 
Operations research uses various methods and tools to model, analyze, and solve problems. Some of the common methods include mathematical programming, simulation, decision analysis, game theory, queuing theory, and network analysis. These methods help to formulate problems in a mathematical way, find optimal or near-optimal solutions using algorithms or heuristics, and evaluate the performance and outcomes of different alternatives.
